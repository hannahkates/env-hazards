Environmental Hazards in NYC

About

New York City has some of the most progressive environmental policies; yet, its century-old buildings, legacy infrastructure, and long industrial history have left many environmental hazards to remediate.

This interactive map compiles open data sources into one consolidated tool, allowing advocates to more easily access this public information.
